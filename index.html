Group Processes

The group worked well overall due to the desire on the part of everybody to collaborate and complete their part of the work. Technical difficulties and lack of availability to participate in some meetings due to other commitments were the main hindrance to collaboration. To mitigate this issue in the future, more clear meeting times in the future and communication through other means outside of meetings were established. Speaking more often in discussions among all team members has also increased after feedback from intra-group review was considered. These changes in the group process allow for greater coordination.

Information Security Consultants are IT professionals responsible for implementing cybersecurity solutions to fulfil the needs of an organisation. Information security consultants have to perform duties to protect a business and educate the people within that business on the proper actions to take in various circumstances. Information security consultants may be required to demonstrate many certifications in addition to their degrees or experience, as their employment position carries a greater risk if performed inadequately.

IT operations managers maintain control over IT services and oversee the functionality of the underlying network infrastructure and systems. Compared to the other roles, they are expected to understand networking and be more involved with hardware maintenance, such as ensuring proper ventilation or working out solutions to power outages

Senior software engineers are highly experienced engineers who develop information systems by being able to resolve complex software issues, understand and build architecture, and can lead software engineering teams. Software engineers are highly diverse in their work and come from diverse backgrounds. Compared to the other roles, their primary responsibility is programming.

Solutions engineers are IT professionals who interface between the business and clients. They have the technology and business skills to help communicate between the parties and implement solutions. The solutions engineer role differs from the others with its higher level of interpersonal and business skills required.

These roles all differ, but the core similarity they share is that they require a lot of communication with others while working with technology. The IT industry has a reputation for being introverted and asocial, but in reality, a significant amount of teamwork and collaboration is involved. The solutions engineer must be capable with talking to clients; the senior software engineer must effectively work with other software engineers, etc.
