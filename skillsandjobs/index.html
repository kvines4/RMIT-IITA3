Skills and Jobs

Interstaters are an innovative fresh start-up looking for enthusiastic and talented individuals to join our team. We have been proposed to develop a revolutionary weightlifting app to help all gym goers and reduce accidents while performing exercises.

Our aim is to bring science to weightlifting and make it available to everyone.

We have several positions available for the upcoming project.

Job

Project Manager

Job Overview

As the Project Manager you will be the interface between the developers and business analyst. You will be responsible for the projects online marketing strategies. You will be working with budget to develop digital marketing campaigns and web applications, identify risks and write plans and reports to describe project state. You should have a solid knowledge of content management systems and SEO practices accompanied by strong interpersonal and communication skills.

Skills

- Understanding of Project Management methodologies and SDLC methodologies

- Ability to manage and present to multiple stakeholders, both technical and non-technical

- A strong knowledge of best practice, with a focus on continual improvement

- Sound understanding of SEO best practices

- Planning and problem solving with product management and delivery teams

- Strong leadership skills including role modelling, coaching, and empowering your team members

- Excellent management, interpersonal, and teambuilding skills

- Positive attitude and deadline-driven

- Tech-savvy

Qualification

- Formal qualifications such as Bachelors in IT or Computer science plus project or product development management certifications

- Take personal responsibility for meeting objectives and progressing work. Demonstrate initiative and proactively steps in and does what is required. Commits energy and drive to see that goals are achieved

Experience

- Ideally 2 or more years demonstrable experience in the management of Software projects including planning and delivering the full project Lifecyle, scope, charter, SOW, schedules, budgets, status reports and PIRs

- Developing detailed designs, plans, and monitoring progress as well as writing up reports

- Practical experience with content management systems, Google Analytics, and project management software

- Proven ability to create and drive strategies for timely project implementation

- Great communication skills and a solid understanding of office collaboration tools like Microsoft Office

Job

Full Stack App developer

Job Overview

As an app developer you will working with the team to bring the project alive. We will be building a mobile application using technologies like computer vison and machine learning. You will bring your experience and help the project change millions of lives to better health

Skills

- Designing and building beautiful and user-friendly applications

- Managing app submission to the App Store and Google Play Store

- Ingesting third party data through API integrations

- Testing and addressing issues

- Proficiency with server-side languages such as Python, Ruby, Java, PHP or .Net.

- Proficiency with fundamental front end languages such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript

- Familiarity with JavaScript frameworks such as Angular JS, React and Amber

- Familiarity with database technology such as MySQL, Oracle and MongoDB

- Solid understanding of mobile application architecture

- Native iOS and Android mobile development experience

- Managing AWS services

Qualification

- Formal qualification such as Bachelors in IT or Computer science or at least 5 years of

Work experience in a similar role with great portfolio

Experience

- Extensive experience as a full stack app developer

- Solid knowledge of .Net and supporting tech

- Knowledge developing AI/ML systems will be highly beneficial

- Excellent grasp of Relational Databases and coding in SQL /Graph

- Experience in UI and UX design

- Experience with Xamarin development

Job

Digital Business Analyst

Job Overview

The Digital Business analyst helps the team to stay on track with projects strategic vison and objectives via utilization of human centred design, collaborating with project manager and following the Lean methodology. This is a crucial role for our small team to help develop a fast MVP and work towards making a market ready application

Skills

- Able to identify business development shortcoming and help find solutions to improve performance

- Clear understanding of application development processes

- Innovative, logic and critical thinking skills

- Have basic knowledge of user interface design principles and user-centric design processes

- Prototype solutions and test new ideas to help team use new solutions

- Summarise information and make clear presentations and reports

- Deep knowledge of digital trends, including Ai, Ml, Saas etc

- Maintain and encourage open and honest business relationships within the team and with business partners to

- ensure the alignment of business objectives

Qualification

A bachelor in IT, Business, Marketing or Web design. Ideally Master of Business Analytics and/or at least 3 years of hands-on experience in digital product management

Experience

- Proven background in digital environments ensuring best practice UX and digital principles are applied to all solutions

- Have a strong background and comfort level in conducting research and then presenting your findings in the

- form of presentations or business cases

- Proven analytical skills with the ability to apply results of analysis

Job

UI/UX designer

Job Overview

As a UI/UX Designer, you will contribute to the overall customer experience vision for the digital environment through designing and delivering user interface for our application. You would be conducting user research and find user needs to allow the application to give the best user experience.

Skills

- Have ninja skills with design and prototyping tools such as Sketch, Figma or Adobe XD

- Use a data driven, customer insights approach to create wireframes, interactive prototypes and validate design hypothesis through usability testing

- Sound understanding of Human Centred Design and Lean UX principles

- Ability to navigate ambiguity and produce quality outcomes

- In-depth understanding of information architecture and user psychology

- Experience conducting moderated and unmoderated usability testing

- Excellent communication, storytelling, and collaboration abilities

- Ability to be pragmatic, design around constraints and adapt to changing requirements

- Ability to be flexible when dealing with changing priorities

Qualification

- Degree qualified or training in Design, Visual Communication, UX or at least 5 years’ experience as UI/UX designer

Experience

- Minimum 2+ years of hands-on experience in UX design, including experience in facilitating workshops, wireframing, prototyping, usability testing and UI design

- Demonstrate an ability to articulate research findings and design decisions in a clear and concise manner to all team members and stakeholders

- Have an awesome online portfolio of UX UI designs including mobile applications

- Experience in using industry standard design tools such as Sketch and Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator)

- Willingness to work in a mixed delivery model environment (Agile, Waterfall, internal and outsourced delivery models)

- Experience working health and fitness industry and sports products as well as interest in and working knowledge of the sports such as weightlifting will be advantageous but not essential
